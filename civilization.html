Civilization

Civilization is a coordination interface. It is not “culture” as decoration; it is coupling as survival. When interfaces drift, collapse follows—quietly, mechanically.
DECLARATION
We do not worship institutions. We test whether coordination matches reality.
We do not moralize collapse. We diagnose drift: incentives, feedback, complexity.
We do not fear acceleration. We fear ungoverned coupling.

Collapse and Drift

INTERFACE CHECK
Input: resources, attention, legitimacy.
Coupling: incentives, rules, enforcement.
Feedback: prices, votes, audits, signals of failure.
Repair: reform, adaptation, institutional refactoring.
Failure: runaway corruption, brittle collapse, irreversible distrust.
