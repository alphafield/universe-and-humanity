Standing on the shoulders of inquiry

For more than two millennia, humanity has asked a small set of fundamental questions.

What is the universe? What is spacetime? What is life? What is information? What is consciousness?

Each era answered part of the question, from its own observational and mathematical horizon.

These answers were not wrong — they were incomplete.

Universe & Humanity does not replace modern science. It asks a different question:

Why do these structures persist at all?

A structural coordinate

We introduce α as a dimensionless structural parameter.

α describes the ratio between coherent structure and disruptive fluctuation in a system.

It does not describe what a system is made of, but whether its structure can persist.

Across observations — from cosmology to biology, from information to intelligence — many phenomena align along a single structural trajectory.

From the origin of the universe, to light, to matter, to life, to mind, to civilization.
