Universe

The universe is treated as the constraint layer that no civilization can negotiate with. Our language here is minimal: structure, interface, stability.
DECLARATION
We do not ask what the universe “means.” We ask what it allows.
We do not inflate complexity. We search for invariants that survive scale.
We do not debate metaphors. We define interfaces: coupling and failure.
